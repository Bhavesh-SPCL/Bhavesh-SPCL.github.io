Our Product : Train Bogie

Engineered for Heavy Loads. Built for Long Hauls.

At Sudarshan Planeta Corporation LLP (SPCL), we specialize in the design and manufacturing of bogie exclusively for freight trains. Our Side Frames and Bolsters are engineered to deliver maximum structural integrity, load-bearing performance, and long-term durability—making them ideal for the demanding conditions of heavy-duty goods transport. We combine high-tech manufacturing with proven engineering to deliver bogies that contribute to freight safety, derailment protection, operational stability, and minimum wear and tear on rail infrastructure.

Integrated Bogie Structure

Our freight bogies are made up of two key components: Side Frames that form the outer frame and the Bolster which carries the vertical load. Together, they form a robust and reliable bogie system capable of hauling large cargo loads across long distances.

Key Features

  • High tensile strength for dynamic loads and shocks
  • Precision machining for perfect alignment
  • Corrosion-resistant finishes for durability
  • Compliant with RDSO specifications
  • Longer service life with reduced wear

Applications

  • Bulk commodity transport
  • Heavy industrial goods & Containerized cargo
  • Specialized defense and infrastructure freight
  • Motor bogies for extremely heavy loads
