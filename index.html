Our Core Product

Bogie Systems: The Foundation of Safe and Efficient Rail Transport

Engineered for Reliability and Performance

The bogie is the critical assembly that supports the railcar body, ensures stability, and provides ride comfort. We specialize in manufacturing high-performance bogie systems engineered to withstand immense operational stresses.

From the high-tensile cast steel frame to the precision-machined components of the suspension system, every element is manufactured to meet and exceed the stringent standards set by the Indian Railways (RDSO) and other global benchmarks.
